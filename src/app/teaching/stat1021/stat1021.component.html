Course Description

This short lecture provides an introduction to how data science is utilized in the industry, with an emphasis on developing skills to facilitate teamwork in software development. In particular, we will sample and analyze the social network of the students in the class and introduce broadly applicable concepts in (static) network analysis. The course will include: an introduction to software development methods and tools; graph theory and graph concepts; community detection; the implications of popular concepts such as "six degrees of separation" and the "friendship paradox".
